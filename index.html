Three years ago I left my parents house to move to San Francisco to "find myself". Having no idea what I wanted to do, or who I really was, I found myself surrounded by the tech boom. So I followed the movement closely; my twitter and RSS feed full. After thorough research I gave it a try through Codecademy. After day one of HTML, I knew I found what I was looking for; this was so me. As I advanced, I couldn't imagine doing anything else with my life. After learning on my own for some time, I began Thinkful, an online full-stack JavaScript program. Which should help me reach my goals of becoming a JavaScript developer. Lucky for me, I picked a booming and recession-proof career path. But for me it's so much more then my earning potential:

Coding helped me discover who I am as a person

it gave me this new passion, not just for programming, but for my life - and it's growing everyday. So check back and follow my blog to see and read about my bootcamp experience and journey to web developer.
