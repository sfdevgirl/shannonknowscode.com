About me
Two years ago I left my parents house to move to San Francisco to "find myself". Having no idea what I wanted to do, or who I really was, I found myself surrounded by "the tech boom". So I followed the "movement" closely; my twitter and RSS feed full. After through research I gave it a try through Codecademy. After day one of HTML, I knew I found what I was looking for; this was so me. As I advanced, I couldn't imagine doing anything else with my life. I took the next step and started an online program through Skillcrush. After finishing HTML & CSS, I was accepted into a rigorous 12 week coding bootcamp, Hacker: Coding Academy. Which should put me on track to start my career as a web developer by 2015. Lucky for me, I picked a booming and recession-proof career path. But for me it's so much more then my earning potential:
Coding helped me discover who I am as person
it gave me this new passion, not just for programming, but for my life - and it's growing everyday. So check back and follow my blog to see and read about my bootcamp experience and journey to web developer.
